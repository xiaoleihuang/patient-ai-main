Our Resources for User Modeling

    • COVID-19 Data
    • We provide a Twitter data related to COVID-19 (Coronavirus). We distribute this Twitter data to support research efforts in the fight against COVID-19. Visit covid19dataresources.org for more COVID-19 related social media and web data resources.
